{{ subtitle }} Working Principle Of Capsule Filling Machine


In manual capsule machine 100 empty capsule is loaded in capsule tray. Capsule tray is placed over the machine bed . Then Capsule position is locked using the knob. With the help of lever upper and lower capsule body is deattached .

Then empty capsule is filled with medicine powder and compressed the medicine powder in capsule with the help of handle. .Then capsule tray is placed over the bed and after unlocking the machine capsule is pressed with handle and lever where upper and lower capsule joined together . After this process with the help of lever capsule tray is unloaded from machine bed and from the capsule tray we unload the filled capsule.

{{ subtitle }} Operating Procedure


  1. Fill empty capsule onto the loading tray and place loading tray over the machine bed
  2. Check the front knob it should be turned to the right.
  3. Pull locking lever forward.It will lock the capsule. Pull down the handle which will lift the capsule upper part and sepratethe capsule
  4. Push locking lever back which will drop down the capsule bodies and become level with filling surface.
  5. Place Powder tray over the bed which keep the powder from spilling
  6. Pour and spread the measured powder. Move the extra powder on the shelf.
  7. Lock the pin plate and compress the powder which will allow more powder to fill inside the capsule.
  8. Return the loading tray place over the bed and unlock the Machine.
  9. Push down the lock handle which will join the capsule and load into the loading tray.
  10. Push down the lock handle again to remove the loading tray.
  11. Capsule are filled now. Remove the capsule from the tray now.
